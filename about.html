Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors Since 1985

The Journey of Dev Raj Sohal Sweets

In 1985, Dev Raj Sohal opened a modest sweet shop in Jalandhar with a simple vision: to share the authentic flavors of traditional Indian sweets with the community. What began as a small family business has blossomed into one of Jalandhar's most beloved destinations for sweets and authentic cuisine.

For nearly four decades, we have upheld the same values that our founder instilled from day one. Every sweet is handcrafted using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Every dish is prepared with the finest ingredients, sourced carefully to ensure authenticity and quality.

Our kitchen is not just a place where food is made—it's where memories are created, traditions are preserved, and love is infused into every creation. From our famous Gulab Jamun to our signature Butter Chicken, each item on our menu tells a story of dedication and passion.
