A Legacy of Taste Since 1985

For nearly four decades, Dev Raj Sohal Sweets & Restaurant has been serving authentic Indian sweets and cuisine to the people of Jalandhar. What started as a small sweet shop has grown into a beloved destination for food lovers.

Our commitment to traditional recipes, quality ingredients, and authentic flavors has remained unchanged. Every dish is prepared with the same care and attention as it was when we first opened our doors.
